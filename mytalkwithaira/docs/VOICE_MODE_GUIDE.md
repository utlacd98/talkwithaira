## Quick Start

### Step 1: Enable Voice Mode
1. Open the chat with Aira
2. Look for the "Voice Mode Off" button above the message input
3. Click it to turn Voice Mode **On**
4. The button will change to "Voice Mode On" with a speaker icon

### Step 2: Speak Your Message
1. Click the **microphone icon** (üé§) next to the send button
2. The input field will pulse with a blue border
3. A red "Recording" indicator will appear
4. **Speak clearly** into your microphone
5. Your words will appear in real-time in the input field
6. Click the microphone again (now red with an X) to stop recording

### Step 3: Send and Listen
1. Review the transcribed text
2. Click the **Send** button (or press Enter)
3. Aira will respond in text
4. **Aira will automatically read her response aloud** in a warm, empathetic voice
5. A "Stop Speaking" button appears while she's talking

### Step 4: Control Playback
- **To stop Aira mid-sentence**: Click the "Stop Speaking" button
- **To turn off voice responses**: Click "Voice Mode On" to toggle it off
- **To use keyboard instead**: Just type normally - voice and text input work together

## Troubleshooting

### "Voice Mode" button doesn't appear
- **Cause**: Your browser doesn't support the Web Speech API
- **Solution**: Use Chrome, Edge, or Safari (latest versions)

### Microphone not working
- **Cause**: Browser doesn't have microphone permission
- **Solution**: 
  1. Click the lock icon in your browser's address bar
  2. Allow microphone access for airasupport.com
  3. Refresh the page

### Transcription is inaccurate
- **Cause**: Background noise, unclear speech, or accent
- **Solution**:
  1. Move to a quieter location
  2. Speak more clearly and at a moderate pace
  3. Use a better microphone
  4. Edit the text manually after transcription

### Aira's voice sounds robotic
- **Cause**: Browser's default voice engine
- **Solution**: 
  - This is normal - browser voices vary by device
  - The voice quality depends on your operating system
  - Future updates may add more voice options

### No sound when Aira speaks
- **Cause**: Volume muted or audio output issue
- **Solution**:
  1. Check your device volume
  2. Check browser isn't muted (tab icon)
  3. Try refreshing the page
  4. Check audio output device settings
